This is my wesbite about Channel Islands Surfboards

These are a few of our most famous boards!

  1. Al Merrick
    • Extremely fast
    • Perfect for 6-10 foot waves
    • Slim body

  2. G Skate
    • Easy to manuever
    • Wider body, less wobbly
    • For wave heights of 2-6 foot waves

  3. Fish Tail
    • Fish tail allows it to turn quickly
    • Light weight and used as an all-around board
    • Used for 2-6 foot high waves
